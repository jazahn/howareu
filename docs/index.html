How Are You (howareu)

A simple, private daily mood check-in app.

What it does

howareu helps you track how you're feeling each day with a quick check-in. Rate your mood, pick the feelings that resonate, and build a personal log over time — all stored in your own Google Sheets spreadsheet.

Key features

  • Quick daily mood rating and feelings check-in
  • Data stored directly in your own Google Drive — you own it completely
  • Optional daily reminder notifications
  • No ads, no analytics, no tracking

Your data, your control

howareu never stores your data on our servers. Everything goes straight to a Google Sheets spreadsheet in your Google Drive. You can view, edit, export, or delete it anytime.
