howareu — Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 27, 2026

Overview

howareu is a personal mood check-in app. Your privacy is important to us. This policy explains what data the app accesses, how it is used, and where it is stored.

Data We Collect

howareu does not collect, transmit, or store your personal data on any server we operate. All data stays in your own Google account.

  • Google Account Info — When you sign in with Google, the app receives your name, email address, and profile photo solely to display within the app.
  • Mood Check-in Responses — Your responses (mood rating and selected feelings) are written directly to a Google Sheets spreadsheet in your own Google Drive. The app does not keep a copy of this data.

Google API Usage

The app requests the following Google permissions:

  • Google Drive (drive.file) — Used exclusively to create and access a "howareu" folder and spreadsheet in your Drive. The app can only access files it creates; it cannot see your other Drive files.
  • Google Sheets (spreadsheets) — Used to read and write mood check-in data to the spreadsheet the app creates.

The app's use of Google user data complies with the Google API Services User Data Policy, including the Limited Use requirements.

Local Storage

The app stores a small amount of data on your device using local storage:

  • Your notification reminder preference and time setting.

This data never leaves your device.

Third-Party Services

The app uses the following third-party services:

  • Google Sign-In — for authentication
  • Google Drive & Sheets APIs — for data storage
  • Expo — as the app development framework

We do not use any analytics, advertising, or tracking services.

Data Sharing

We do not sell, share, or transfer your data to any third parties. Your mood data exists only in your own Google Drive.

Data Retention & Deletion

Since all data is stored in your own Google Drive, you have full control over it. You can view, edit, or delete your data at any time by accessing the "howareu" folder in Google Drive. Uninstalling the app removes all locally stored preferences. To revoke the app's access to your Google account, visit your Google Account permissions.

Children's Privacy

howareu is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Changes will be reflected on this page with an updated date.

Contact
